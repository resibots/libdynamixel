Decodes the content of a status packet received from the servos

This method is only used by the StatusPacket class, to make it independent of the protocol version.

Parameters
packetcontent of the received packet
idid of the sending actuator
parametersparameters of the status packet, filled by unpack_status
throw_exceptionsboolean telling to throw exceptions if the packet is malformed

the state of the packet unpacking
